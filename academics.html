Academic Programmes

Providing quality education from Junior to Senior Secondary School.

Junior Secondary School (JSS)

JSS 1

Foundation classes with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, ICT and moral education.

JSS 2

Students continue to develop knowledge and practical skills in all core subjects.

JSS 3

Preparation for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).

Senior Secondary School (SSS)

SSS 1

Students choose their preferred department and begin specialized studies.

SSS 2

Advanced classroom and laboratory learning with continuous assessment.

SSS 3

Intensive preparation for WAEC, NECO and UTME examinations.

Departments

Science Department

Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Further Mathematics and Computer Studies.

Commercial Department

Economics, Commerce, Financial Accounting and Office Practice.

Arts Department

Government, Literature in English, CRS/IRS, Civic Education and History.
