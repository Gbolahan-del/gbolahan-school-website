Admissions

Join Gbolahan Comprehensive High School and become part of our success story.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Admission Application Form.
  • Birth Certificate.
  • Two Recent Passport Photographs.
  • Previous School Result.
  • Transfer Certificate (if applicable).

Admission Process

Step 1

Obtain and complete the admission application form.

Step 2

Submit the completed form with the required documents.

Step 3

Attend the entrance examination and oral interview.

Step 4

Successful applicants will receive an admission letter.
