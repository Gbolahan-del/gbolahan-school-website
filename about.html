About Our School

Learn more about Gbolahan Comprehensive High School.

Our History

Gbolahan Comprehensive High School was established to provide quality education and build students with sound moral values, leadership skills and academic excellence. Over the years, the school has produced outstanding students who excel in various fields of study and society.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading secondary schools known for academic excellence, discipline and innovation.

Our Mission

To provide quality education in a safe and friendly learning environment while developing responsible future leaders.

Our Core Values

  • Discipline
  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Respect
  • Teamwork
  • Innovation

Principal's Message

At Gbolahan Comprehensive High School, we believe every child has the potential to succeed. Our dedicated teachers work tirelessly to ensure that students receive quality education, moral guidance and the confidence to face future challenges.
